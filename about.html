Hololive indonesia is a female Vtuber group with an Indonesian branch in 2019 after English and Japanese branches. like most other VTubers, they also livestream activities such as playing games, interacting with viewers, producing original songs, and collaborating with other VTubers.

Generations

AREA15 is the name of the first generation of hololive indonesia consisting of risu, moona, and iofi. the name area15 is taken from the name of their collaboration event which is held on the 15th of each month. the reason it is called area15 is that they (risu, moona, and iofi) have birthdays on the same date, namely 15 in different months.

Holoro is the name of the second generation of hololive indonesia which consists of ollie, anya, and reine. this holoro designation is a combination of the words "hololive" and "loro" aka 2 in Javanese.

HOLOH3RO is the name of the third generation of hololive indonesia consisting of zeta, kaela, and kobo. the same as the designation holoro, the word holoh3roini is based on the words "hololive", "hero", and the number 3. which means they are "everyday heroes" who represent generation 3 of hololive ID.
